About MOJAF

Materials That Keep Saudi Projects Moving

Founded in 2020, MOJAF is a Saudi-owned distributor supplying SPC and vinyl flooring, PS & MDF skirting, wall panels, insulation, doors, fasteners and electrical accessories. We support mega and mid-sized projects with stocked inventory, engineered solutions and coordinated deliveries.

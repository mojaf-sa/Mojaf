FAQ

What is SPC flooring best used for?

SPC flooring is commonly used in offices, retail, residential, clinics and modular spaces where dimensional stability and water resistance are useful.

Does MOJAF support LVT flooring Riyadh enquiries?

Yes. MOJAF can discuss LVT flooring Riyadh requirements including wear layer, design, trims and delivery details.

Is vinyl flooring waterproof?

Many vinyl and SPC products offer strong water resistance, but installation quality, subfloor preparation and edge treatment still matter.

What should I check before ordering flooring?

Confirm area, wastage, thickness, wear layer, color, underlay or adhesive, trims, skirting and delivery schedule.

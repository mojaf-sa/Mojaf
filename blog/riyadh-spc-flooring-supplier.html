  • Confirm total thickness (4.0 mm vs 5.0 mm), wear layer (0.3–0.7 mm) and click system profile. Most Riyadh office projects prefer 5 mm planks with IXPE underlay attached.

Moisture, substrate and acoustic preparation

SPC flooring tolerates minor subfloor variation, yet Riyadh projects still face HVAC delays that trap moisture. Schedule calcium chloride or in-situ RH tests at least two weeks before installation. For ground floors and parking podiums, specify a 200-micron vapour barrier beneath the underlay and inspect for cracks exceeding 3 mm. Where footfall noise is a concern—especially mixed-use towers—upgrade to IXPE or EVA-backed planks and consider perimeter sealant to minimise flanking sound.

Installation essentials

  1. Acclimatise planks 24 hours on site and inspect locking profiles before deployment.
  2. Stage tools—tapping blocks, pull bars, guillotine cutters—and allocate waste factors of 5–8% for patterned layouts.
  3. Seal expansion gaps (8–10 mm) with colour-matched silicone where skirting cannot cover gaps, especially at sliding doors.

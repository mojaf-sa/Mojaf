How do I choose between galvanized and stainless screws?

Use galvanized finishes for many indoor and protected applications, while stainless or higher corrosion-resistant options are preferred for coastal, wet or exposed conditions.

What should I send for a screw quotation?

Share substrate, screw size or sample, quantity, finish, delivery city, drawings if available and any consultant specification.

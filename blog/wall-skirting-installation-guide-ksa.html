Wall Skirting Installation Guide KSA

By MOJAF • Updated 2025-11-05

Wall skirting protects finishes from trolley impact, cleaning machines and daily wear. MOJAF supplies PVC, PS and MDF profiles with matching corners and end caps. This guide explains how to select the right material and execute a clean installation sequence for Saudi Arabia fit-out projects.

MaterialWhen to useHighlights
PVCHealthcare, wet areasWaterproof, easy to clean, integrates with vinyl coves
PS (polystyrene)Hotels, retail, residentialImpact resistant, pre-finished colours, waterproof
MDFDry interiorsFoil-wrapped, paintable, budget friendly

Pre-installation checklist

  • Confirm wall plumbness and mark finished floor levels after flooring installation.
  • Plan expansion joints for runs longer than 10 m, especially with MDF profiles.
  • Order inside/outside corners, end caps and colour-matched sealant with the skirting.

Installation steps

  1. Cut profiles to length using a mitre saw for precise joints.
  2. Apply adhesive beads or use fasteners and clips as recommended by the manufacturer.
  3. Press the profile firmly against the wall, ensuring consistent alignment along the floor.
  4. Seal joints and terminations with colour-matched silicone or caulk.

Coordinate with flooring packages

Schedule skirting installation after vinyl, SPC or tile works are complete to ensure consistent heights. Combine orders with SPC & LVT flooring and wall & floor skirting accessories to reduce freight costs and maintain colour consistency.

Maintenance recommendations

PVC and PS profiles can be cleaned with neutral detergents; avoid abrasive pads. MDF skirting should be protected from standing water and inspected for chips during housekeeping rounds.

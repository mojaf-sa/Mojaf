Vinyl Flooring Buyer’s Guide KSA

By MOJAF • Updated 2025-11-05

Vinyl sheet and LVT flooring continue to be the fastest-moving finishes for hospitality, education and healthcare projects across Saudi Arabia. Use this guide to evaluate technical requirements, coordinate deliveries and match accessories so that submittals, BOQs and site teams stay aligned.

Popular constructionsHeterogeneous and homogeneous sheet, dry-back and click LVT (2.5–5.5 mm)
Wear layers0.3 mm (residential), 0.5–0.7 mm (commercial/high traffic)
Stock citiesRiyadh, Jeddah, Dammam — weekly inbound replenishment

1. Confirm technical specifications

  • Align with BOQ on total thickness, wear layer, slip rating (R9/R10) and fire classification.
  • Match colour references against approved mockups; request sample boards for client sign-off.
  • Check subfloor tolerances (usually <3 mm variation over 2 m) and specify primer/skim coat where needed.

2. Moisture & substrate preparation

Vinyl flooring adhesives require controlled moisture. Conduct moisture tests (CM or RH) before shipment and sequence screed drying with HVAC commissioning. For healthcare projects, confirm cove-form skirting and weld rods are available to maintain infection-control requirements.

3. Accessory checklist

  • SPC & LVT Flooring kits — matching trims, reducers and stair nosings.
  • Wall & Floor Skirting — PVC/PS skirting to protect wet areas and corridors.
  • Fasteners & Hardware — self-tapping screws and anchors for transition profiles.
  • Vinyl sheet adhesives — primers, weld rods and coving tools available ex-stock.

4. Delivery & logistics planning

  • Standard colors ship within 1–3 days from Riyadh or Dammam warehouses; specialty patterns may require 3–5 weeks.
  • Plan phased deliveries by floor or zone to reduce on-site storage and avoid damage.
  • Include installation consumables (adhesive, rollers, cove trims) in the same shipment to prevent delays.

5. Documentation & compliance

MOJAF provides EN 13501 fire reports, VOC declarations and test data on request. Submit install method statements for main contractors and ensure warranties remain valid by using approved adhesives and trained installers.

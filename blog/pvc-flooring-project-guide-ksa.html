PVC Flooring Project Guide KSA

By MOJAF • Updated 2025-11-05

PVC flooring is the preferred finish for high-traffic, hygiene-critical zones like clinics, laboratories and schools. Modular builders also rely on PVC rolls for lightweight, fast installations. This guide summarises specification requirements, accessory coordination and maintenance practices that MOJAF supports across Saudi Arabia.

Thickness range2.0 mm – 3.0 mm homogeneous / 2.0 mm + wear layer for heterogeneous
Surface protectionPUR / UV coatings for reduced maintenance, anti-bacterial additives
AccessoriesCoved skirting, weld rods, stair nosings, transition profiles

Key specification questions

  • Does the room require homogeneous sheet for hospital or lab certification, or is heterogeneous sufficient?
  • What slip resistance (R9–R11) is mandated for wet rooms or kitchens?
  • Is static control (ESD) needed for server rooms or diagnostic suites?

Installation workflow

  1. Prepare the subfloor with self-levelling compound and approved primer.
  2. Dry-fit sheets and pre-form coves using hot-air guns and cove strips.
  3. Apply low-VOC adhesive, roll out air pockets, then hot weld seams after curing.

Pair with complementary products

  • Vinyl Sheet & Sports Flooring — colour-matched weld rods and adhesives.
  • Wall & Floor Skirting — PS and PVC skirting for healthcare coves.
  • Portacabin Materials — insulation, panels and fittings for modular builds.

Maintenance schedule

Implement a maintenance plan with daily dust mopping, weekly damp mopping and quarterly machine scrubbing using neutral pH cleaners. Apply PU refresh coatings annually to extend surface life. Document cleaning protocols for facility management audits.

Documentation ready for submittals

MOJAF supplies data sheets, MSDS, fire ratings and cleaning manuals in English and Arabic. Share room-by-room schedules and delivery milestones to align shipments with installation crews.

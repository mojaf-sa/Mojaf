Products & Specs

For flooring we stock rigid SPC with dense core stability, commercial wear layers, textured surfaces, matching trims and stair noses. Our skirting catalog covers MDF (primed/painted), PS and PVC options with inside/outside corners, end caps and color bonding. Fasteners include stainless and galvanized screws, anchors, washers and threaded rods. Board lines include gypsum (standard/MR/FR), cement and fiber-cement substrates. Insulation products prioritize acoustic attenuation and fire classifications used in residential, retail and modular builds.

Each shipment is QA-checked with batch references. Typical documentation includes COA/COC on request. Palletization and protective wrapping minimize site waste and damage. For contractors, our quotes can bundle accessories to reduce store runs and change-orders. We coordinate logistics to Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam/Khobar and nationwide routes.

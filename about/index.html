About MOJAF

End-to-end building material supply partner for contractors, developers and facility managers across Saudi Arabia.

Who We Are

Saudi Distribution Network with Project-Driven Support

MOJAF launched in Jeddah in 2020 to simplify procurement for interior fit-out, hospitality, residential and infrastructure projects. With branches in Riyadh, Dammam, Jubail and Madinah, we keep stock close to major job sites and coordinate fast deliveries with the documentation contractors need.

Our technical sales team reviews BOQs, recommends suitable specifications and coordinates approvals with consultants. Once material selections are confirmed, our logistics team plans phased drops, ensuring on-site crews receive the right quantities at the right time.
